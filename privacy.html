Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2023

At HugoNest, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

We may collect personally identifiable information, such as:

  • Email address (when you create an account or contact us)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • OAuth tokens (for connecting to Git providers)

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage information, such as:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App interactions (features used, time spent)
  • Error logs and performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis to improve our service
  • To monitor the usage of our service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

Your Content

HugoNest is designed with a "zero-knowledge" approach to your content. We do not store your Hugo website content on our servers. All content remains on your device and is transmitted directly to your Git repository through secure connections.

When you use our AI features, text is temporarily processed by our AI service providers to generate suggestions or translations, but is not permanently stored.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your information with third parties in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may employ third-party companies to facilitate our Service, provide the Service on our behalf, perform Service-related services, or assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf.
  • Git Providers: When you connect to GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket, we facilitate the connection using OAuth protocols.
  • Analytics: We may use third-party Service Providers to monitor and analyze the use of our Service.
  • AI Processing: When you use our AI-powered features, your content is processed by our AI partners.

Security

We value your trust in providing us your personal information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

We implement the following security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • OAuth for secure authentication without storing credentials
  • Regular security audits and tests
  • Encrypted local storage of sensitive information

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to withdraw consent
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@xgdebug.com.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: xgdebug@outlook.com
  • By visiting the contact page on our website
