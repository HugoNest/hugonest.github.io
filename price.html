Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and subscription

Do I need to buy the app before subscribing to AI features?

Yes, the base app purchase ($0.99) is required to use HugoNest. The AI subscription is an additional feature set that enhances the core functionality.

Can I use HugoNest without the AI subscription?

Absolutely! The basic app purchase gives you access to all the essential features needed to manage your Hugo site, including the Markdown editor, Git integration, and more.

How is the yearly subscription billed?

The yearly subscription is billed as a single payment of $9.99 , offering over 15% savings compared to the monthly subscription. It automatically renews after 12 months unless canceled.

Can I cancel my AI subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your AI subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access to the AI features until the end of your current billing period. After that, you'll still have full access to all the basic features of the app.

Is there a free trial for the AI features?

New users can try the AI features with a limited number of requests to experience the benefits before subscribing. Check the app for current trial offers.
